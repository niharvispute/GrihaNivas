About the Project

Lodha Palava The Crown is the latest masterpiece from Lodha Group, situated in the thriving Dombivali East corridor. With four iconic towers rising 32 floors, this project redefines urban living with meticulously designed apartments across 1, 2, and 3 BHK configurations.

Set across 12 acres of curated landscape, the project offers over 40 world-class amenities including an Olympic-sized pool, a fully equipped fitness centre, a vibrant clubhouse, and dedicated children's activity zones — all designed to elevate daily life.
